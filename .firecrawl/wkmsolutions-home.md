# WKM SOLUTIONS

Building Diversity, Executing Excellence

# Delivering smart, practical solutions with a strong commitment to our community.

**WKM Solutions, LLC** is a full-service general contractor and major subcontractor specializing in renovation, modernization, design-build, and new construction. Since 2011, we’ve provided experienced, knowledgeable professionals to manage every phase of large-scale projects, with a strong focus on public school remodeling. Founded by two brothers who have worked across all trades of construction, WKM is capable of delivering contracts up to $25 million, from new builds to complex renovations. Our management team guides clients through every step to ensure on-time delivery, strict safety standards, and exceptional quality. We don’t just improve buildings—we improve the lives of the people who use them.
