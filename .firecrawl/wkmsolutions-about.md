## About WKM Solutions

WKM Solutions, LLC is a minority owned company based in D.C. and is a general contractor that offers a range of building services to clients in the DMV area. They specialize in construction project management, handling projects up to $25 million in value, and have experience in both renovation and new construction. The company has a professional management team that ensures quality workmanship, efficient execution, and effective project management throughout. They have a network of reliable local subcontractors, designers, quality and testing consultants, enabling them to fulfill the needs of their clients. WKM Solutions has extensive experience working with DC Public Schools and the DC government, and they plan to develop relationships with all DC government agencies to build trust with their clients.

## Our History

WKM Solutions is a certified minority company created by two brothers with a high level of design and construction experience.

The Matos brothers have worked diligently on new construction and remodeling residential and commercial buildings. For the past 15 years, the principals have worked on all areas of renovation and design-build for DGS with great emphasis on DC Public Schools and in the State of Maryland. They have acquired great experience in planning and delivering fast track summer projects along with emergency On-Call response services. WKM has provided services for many DC agencies, such as DC Public Schools, Dept. of General Services (DGS), DC Public Library (DPL), Department of Recs (DPR) and DC Dept. of Youth Rehabilitation Services and in Maryland as well, Prince Georges County Public School (PGCPS), Frederick County, and Montgomery County…

We know what the government agencies expects from any contractor and is ready to deliver that excellent and efficient service. We are proud to always deliver our jobs on time without any problems or delays.

**WKM Solutions** is familiar with the local requirements, codes, licenses and process needed to keep the job site functioning properly. WKM knows the jobs must be completed quickly and is prepared to meet any given deadlines. In 2015, WKM were awarded roughly $2,200,000.00 in contracts from DGS; roughly $4,000,000.00 in 2016 and roughly $7,000,000.00 in 2017. All work has been completed on time and on budget. The multiple current On-call services contract are still ongoing. It’s WKM policy to be prompted to respond to any task assigned by our client and we always avoid change orders and schedule delays.

WKM Solutions’ primary goal of meeting customers’ construction needs through successful relationships with owner management teams is evidenced by our expanding base of repeat clients. WKM offers preconstruction services, job site and project management, administrative support, and document control. WKM evaluates projects for constructability, provides cost estimates, helps maintain project scheduling, and works with owners to meet quality and time objectives.

WKM also suggests cost effective means of construction and help select the best subcontractors for each project. Once a project has begun, WKM Solutions maintains full-time superintendents who are responsible for all daily logs, reviewing quality and timeliness of subcontractors, conducting safety meetings and maintaining good communications with the client.

WKM owners have over 15 years of experience in project management and we only work with the most competent and experienced subcontractors, offering apprenticeship and internship opportunities.
